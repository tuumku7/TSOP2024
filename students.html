STUDENT AWARDS

One of the great efforts that TSOP makes is to encourage and reward students of the highest caliber to attend and be apart of our society. One of the ways we do this is to heavily discount registration fees to our meetings. Another way is to give awards for excellence.


During the conference TSOP judges will be awarding the Danielle Kondla Best Student Presentation Award. Usually two awards, $300 for the oral presentation and $200 for the poster presentation, are given during TSOP conference closing ceremony. Student-author must deliver the oral presentation to qualify for the Best Oral Presentation Award. Student-author of the poster must deliver the presentation of his/her poster to qualify for the Best Poster Presentation Award.
