40th TSOP ANNUAL MEETING
Decoding the shift: Organics and Critical Minerals in Future Energy

Abstract submission deadline July 8th!

Welcome everyone to the 40th Meeting of The Society for Organic Petrology!
It’s VIRTUAL and it’s going to be GREAT!

Join us between the 14th and 22th of September 2024 for two days of a Short Course and three days of Technical Talks, we even have a very special Roundtable Discusson.

We are looking for submissions for talks and posters from geoscientists who are interested in any aspect of organic material: from carbon credits, to uses in forensics or paleobotany to characterisation of critical elements or basinal tectonics.
